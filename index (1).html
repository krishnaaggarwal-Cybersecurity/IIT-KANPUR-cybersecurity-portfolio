Security Writeups

Why Classical Ciphers Fail: A Cryptanalysis Perspective

Frequency analysis on Caesar (broken in ≤25 guesses), Kasiski examination on Vigenère (key length recovery via PoV spacing), key-reuse attacks on XOR. The progression from substitution ciphers to AES — every step motivated by the attack that broke the previous design.

LSB Steganography: How It Works, How It's Used, How It's Detected

Full technical explanation of LSB embedding in 24-bit BMP images, capacity calculations, BMP format internals. Real APT group usage (Turla, APT28). Detection methods: chi-square attack, RS analysis, StegExpose, zsteg. Why JPEG lossy compression destroys LSB payloads and how steganographers work around it.
